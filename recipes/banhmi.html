Banh Mi Recipe

Banh mi is one of the most widely popular vietnamese foods.

It is incredibly simple to make and amazingly delicious!

Ingredients

  • French Baguette
  • Pork Belly
  • Sugar
  • Fish sauce
  • Garlic
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Cilantro
  • Pate
  • Thinly sliced cucumber
  • Diced carrots
  • Diced daikon
  • Jalepeno Peppers

Steps

  1. Cut the French baguette in half.
  2. Marinate the pork with sugar,fish sauce,and finely chopped garlic. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Cut the daikon and carrot into thin strips.
  4. Pour rice vinegar,water,and sugar over the daikon and carrot and allow it to stand there for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Cut the Jalepeno pepper into thin slices.
  6. Cook the marinated pork in a pan until both sides are browned and crispy.
  7. Spread the Pate on one side of the baguette and assemble the sliced pork followed by the carrots,daikon,cilantro,and the jalepeno peppers.
  8. Place the other half of the baguette on top and enjoy!
